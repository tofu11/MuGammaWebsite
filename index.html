About Us

Theta Tau, founded in 1904, is the largest and oldest co-ed engineering fraternity in the U.S. We foster brotherhood, professionalism, and service among future engineers, offering a supportive community for building lifelong friendships and leadership skills. With networking events, workshops, and service projects, Theta Tau helps students succeed academically and professionally. Join us to uphold integrity, teamwork, and engineering excellence.
